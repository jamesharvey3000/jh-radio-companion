How to Get Direct URLs

YouTube
Paste any YouTube URL and hit Add. Audio is extracted automatically via Piped — no account needed. Note: stream URLs expire after a few hours so prep your crate close to your set time.
Dropbox
Upload your MP3, click Share → Copy link. Change ?dl=0 to ?dl=1 at the end of the URL.
Google Drive
Share file as Anyone with link. Copy the file ID from the URL and use:
https://drive.google.com/uc?export=download&id=FILE_ID
Stream URL
Any direct .mp3, .ogg, or Icecast/Shoutcast stream URL works directly.
